About Marvel

Marvel Comics is an American media and entertainment company best known for its comic books and superhero characters, including Spider-Man, Iron Man, Captain America, and many others. Founded in 1939, Marvel has grown into one of the most influential forces in the entertainment industry, producing blockbuster films, TV shows, and more.
